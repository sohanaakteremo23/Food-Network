About Me

My name is Ms. Eto and I am the creator of this website. I have a strong passion for food made by hand using traditional methods from high quality ingredients. I have been making my own breads, sausages, cheeses, beer, ciders and other artisan foods for nearly two decades now. It may come as a surprise but making these foods at home is not that difficult. It certainly is a lot simpler than most people think.
